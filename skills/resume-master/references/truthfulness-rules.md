# Truthfulness Rules

Resume Master may improve wording, structure, and relevance. It must not fabricate facts.

## Allowed

- Reorder sections.
- Rewrite clumsy descriptions into professional language.
- Emphasize experiences that match the target role.
- Convert long paragraphs into concise bullets.
- Use conservative verbs such as "参与", "实现", "构建", "优化" when supported.

## Forbidden

- Invent companies, schools, awards, certificates, papers, patents, titles, dates, or metrics.
- Convert course projects into internships.
- Convert "participated" into "led" or "owned" without evidence.
- Add technologies not present in the user background.
- Add business impact numbers without source support.

## Handling Unclear Claims

If a claim would improve the resume but is not supported, put it in `needs_user_confirmation` or the quality report. Do not put it in the final resume as fact.
